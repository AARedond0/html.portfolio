Alex's best movies of all time

My top three favorites

Troy

Starring: Brad Pitt, Eric Bana, and Orlando Bloom.
An all star casts, retelling the story of The Iliad.
An epic battle of massive proportion, spectacular
cinematography, and a stunning show of a sturdy,
muscular physique of Brad as the Greek warrior Achilles.

A Winter's Tale

Starring: Collin Farell, Jessica Findlay, Russel Crowe,
William Hurt, and Jennifer Connelly.
A story of miracle. You or me, a miracle to someone.
A story of undying love, an everlasting love. A battle between good
and evil, where good always prevails.

An Interview with God

Starring: Brenton Thwaites, and David Strathairn.
This movie attempts to present God as all powerful and everloving God.
How He works in mysterious ways, and that His will and human
free will are not contradictory because they are not
two version of the same thing, but rather, they fit together perfectly.
It addresses one of the most common question
both believers and non believers ask;
'why do bad things happen to good people?'
This movie is well written and I highly recommend to anyone.
